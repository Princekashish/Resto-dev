Our Team

Chef Jean-Paul

A French-born chef with over 20 years of experience, Jean-Paul is known for his classic French cuisine and his use of fresh, locally sourced ingredients. He has trained under some of the most renowned chefs in France and has worked in some of the best restaurants in Paris and London.

Chef Maria

A Mexican-American chef, Maria combines traditional Mexican flavors with modern cooking techniques to create unique and delicious dishes. She has been featured in several food magazines and has made appearances on several cooking shows. Maria has won many awards for her contributions to the culinary industry.

Chef Ahmed

A Middle Eastern chef, Ahmed specializes in traditional dishes from his home country of Lebanon. He has a passion for using fresh herbs and spices to create bold and flavorful dishes. Ahmed has worked in several prestigious restaurants in the Middle East and has recently opened his own restaurant in the United States.

Chef Hiro

A Japanese chef, Hiro is known for his expert sushi and sashimi creations. He trained in Japan for several years under some of the best sushi chefs in the world, and has brought his skills to the United States. Hiro's restaurant is considered one of the best sushi restaurants in the country.
